Critical infrastructure and defense sectors are increasingly deploying agentic AI systems to support mission-critical systems and capitalize on significant automation benefits. However, these systems can introduce additional cybersecurity risks, such as an expanded attack surface, privilege creep, behavioral misalignment, and obscure event records.
