A New York Cop Got Injured at a Boxing Match. Now Madison Square Garden Is Banning His Lawyer

Posted on May 20, 2026

A New York police officer, John Przybyszewski, was injured while working a private security gig at Madison Square Garden during a boxing match. The officer, a seven-year veteran of the NYPD, was knocked to the ground and pinned beneath several people during a scuffle involving the rapper Lil Tjay and his entourage. Przybyszewski claims to have suffered significant cervical and lumbar spine injuries, some of which are permanent, and has sued both Lil Tjay and Madison Square Garden. His lawyer, John Scola, who frequently represents NYPD officers in disputes, filed the lawsuit in February of this year, alleging that the Garden’s operational decisions placed Przybyszewski in harm’s way.
