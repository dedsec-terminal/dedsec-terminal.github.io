Rassvet is often compared to SpaceX's Starlink, as both projects aim to provide high-speed internet access with low latency. However, experts note that Rassvet's terminals are larger and heavier than those used by Starlink, which may limit the network's capabilities.

The Rassvet project has been described as a dual-use system, with both civilian and military applications. The launch of the satellites was carried out by the Russian Defense Ministry, and the project has received significant funding from the Russian Ministry of Communications. The system's design and orbital configuration suggest that it is intended to serve institutional and corporate customers in remote or hard-to-reach regions, including areas with limited access to traditional internet infrastructure.
