**The Fragmented State of Modern Enterprise Identity**
Modern enterprise identity is becoming increasingly fragmented, with identity activity spread across thousands of applications, decentralized teams, machine identities, and autonomous systems. This has led to "Identity Dark Matter," where nearly half of enterprise identity activity occurs outside the visibility of centralized Identity and Access Management (IAM) systems. As a result, security teams are unable to see the full scope of identity activity, creating a widening gap between perceived and actual access.

**Defining the Identity Visibility and Intelligence Platform (IVIP) Category**
To address this issue, Gartner has introduced the Identity Visibility and Intelligence Platform (IVIP) category, which provides an independent layer of oversight above access management and governance. An IVIP solution rapidly ingests and unifies IAM data, leveraging AI-driven analytics to provide a single window into identity events, user-resource relationships, and posture. A credible IVIP must serve as an active intelligence engine, providing continuous discovery of human and non-human identities, unifying fragmented information, and delivering intelligence using analytics and AI.

**Orchid Security's IVIP Solution and Implementation Roadmap**
Orchid Security operationalizes the IVIP model by transforming fragmented identity signals into continuous, application-level intelligence. Their solution provides visibility and data scope, data unification, and intelligence, enabling organizations to discover, unify, and analyze identity activity across systems. To reduce the attack surface, IAM leaders are recommended to form a cross-disciplinary task force, perform risk-quantified gap analysis, implement no-code remediation, and leverage unified visibility for high-stakes events. By implementing an IVIP solution, organizations can shrink their attack surface, reduce identity risk, and move towards a more secure and governed identity ecosystem.
