The term "vCISO platform" was once used to describe the tools and software used by Managed Service Providers (MSPs) to build their cybersecurity practices. However, the work has outgrown this descriptor, and a more precise name is now needed: the Security Growth Platform. This platform combines security program management, CISO-grade decision intelligence, multi-tenant portfolio architecture, and revenue intelligence in one system. It is designed to support the unique needs of MSPs and Managed Security Service Providers (MSSPs) who are delivering security services to small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs).

The demand for cybersecurity services has grown significantly, with SMB cybersecurity spending projected to reach $109 billion in 2026. However, the tools and platforms available to MSPs have not kept pace with this growth. Traditional Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) platforms are designed for enterprise compliance and do not meet the needs of MSPs. vCISO tools, on the other hand, are designed for single advisory engagements and lack the compliance and automation depth needed by MSPs. Enterprise-first compliance platforms compete with the channel and do not provide the partner-only delivery and revenue intelligence needed by MSPs.

The Security Growth Platform tier is a new category that addresses the gaps in the existing market. It is defined by five key capabilities: CISO Intelligence, unified security, risk, and compliance, complete security lifecycle management, portfolio-level revenue intelligence, and a multi-tenant architecture designed for MSP and MSSP scale. This platform is designed to support the unique needs of MSPs and MSSPs, providing them with the tools and infrastructure needed to deliver security services to their clients. It enables them to scale their security practices, grow their revenue, and provide better outcomes for their clients. The Security Growth Platform tier is a distinct category that is separate from traditional GRC platforms, vCISO tools, and enterprise-first compliance platforms.
