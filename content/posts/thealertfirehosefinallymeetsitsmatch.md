The reputation of Network Detection and Response (NDR) systems has long been plagued by complaints of being "noisy" and generating too much data. However, with the integration of agentic AI capabilities, NDR has evolved to catch threats earlier, triage faster, and reduce false positives. This shift is due to AI's ability to autonomously fetch data, triage alerts, and perform correlation and initial analysis, handling the time-consuming and repetitive work that previously overwhelmed analysts.

The introduction of agentic AI has transformed the vast amounts of data generated by NDR systems from a liability to a strategic asset. By analyzing thousands of data points simultaneously, AI can identify connections between low-severity activities that might otherwise go unnoticed, surfacing detections that might have been missed. This enables analysts to focus on high-priority threats, while AI handles the tedious tasks and provides a complete, correlated story from network data. Additionally, AI's correlation capabilities reduce the need for manual tuning, which was a common struggle in traditional NDR deployments.

The benefits of NDR with agentic AI are clear when compared to traditional NDR systems. With AI handling triage, analysts are presented with prioritized detections, complete with relevant evidence and suggested response actions. Proper deployment, including baselining, staying tuned, and SOC integration, is still essential to getting the most out of NDR. By leveraging high-quality data and integrating with other AI-powered SOC tools, organizations can unlock the full potential of NDR and finally keep pace with the evolving threat landscape. With Corelight's NDR platform, which combines deep visibility with agentic AI and advanced detections, organizations can trust their NDR system to deliver better visibility and faster response.
