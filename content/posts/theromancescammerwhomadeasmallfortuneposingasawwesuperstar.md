Chibuike, a young man from Ikotun, Nigeria, was struggling to make a living, working as a dancer and trying various online scams without much success. He decided to become a "Yahoo Boy," a term used to describe Nigerian romance scammers, and began learning the basics of the trade. After trying different approaches, Chibuike discovered that impersonating a celebrity, specifically WWE wrestler Cody Rhodes, was a lucrative strategy. He created a fake online persona, using edited photos and scripted messages to convince his victims that he was the real Cody Rhodes.

Chibuike's big break came when he connected with Theresa, a lonely woman from Ireland who was obsessed with Cody Rhodes. He used his charm and scripted messages to gain her trust, and soon she was sending him money, thinking that she was helping her beloved wrestler. Chibuike's strategy was to overwhelm Theresa with affectionate words and attention, making her feel special and loved. He used various excuses, known as "billing" in Yahoo Boys slang, to ask for money, including pretending that Cody Rhodes' bank account was frozen due to his divorce and that he needed money for medical treatments and other expenses. Theresa, smitten with her online lover, sent Chibuike hundreds of euros, which he used to improve his lifestyle and buy luxury items.

As Chibuike's scam continued to succeed, he refined his tactics, using his own words and emotions to manipulate Theresa. He even bought her a ring and sent it to her house, further solidifying their online relationship. Chibuike's story is a fascinating and disturbing look into the world of Nigerian romance scammers, who use the internet to exploit vulnerable people and make a living. Through his experiences, Chibuike learned that the key to being a successful romance scammer is to give his victims attention and make them feel loved, and he used this strategy to extract thousands of euros from Theresa.
