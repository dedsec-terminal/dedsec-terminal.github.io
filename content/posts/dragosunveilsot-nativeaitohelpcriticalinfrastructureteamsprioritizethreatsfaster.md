Here are three concise paragraphs summarizing the article:

Dragos has introduced EmberAI, an OT-native AI designed to help critical infrastructure teams prioritize threats more efficiently. Built on the Dragos Intelligence Fabric, EmberAI provides analysts with immediate access to OT-specific intelligence, enabling them to gain detailed visibility into assets, vulnerabilities, and network activity. This allows teams to prioritize threats by operational impact and make informed decisions based on real adversary data.

The need for OT-native AI is growing as threat activity against critical infrastructure accelerates, and the shortage of professionals with the necessary skills to address these complex threats widens. Existing tools often prioritize visibility over understanding, and general-purpose AI lacks the operational context to distinguish critical exposures from background noise. EmberAI addresses this gap by providing OT-specific intelligence and expertise, empowering analysts to see, understand, and act with confidence, regardless of their experience level.

EmberAI is powered by the Dragos Intelligence Fabric, which is built on over five petabytes of daily OT telemetry, 10-plus years of adversary tracking, and frontline incident response experience. The platform operates on a principle of OT-specific intelligence applied in context, distinguishing it from generic AI. EmberAI features an intelligence-driven query engine, contextual correlation, adversary-informed guidance, and workflow acceleration, among other capabilities. Its design principles prioritize analyst control, transparency, and audibility, ensuring that customer data remains secure and within the customer's environment.
