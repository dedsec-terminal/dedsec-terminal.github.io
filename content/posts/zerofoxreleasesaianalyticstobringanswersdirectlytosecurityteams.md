ZeroFox has launched a new platform capability called ZeroFox AI Analytics, which provides security teams with real-time visibility into their external threat landscape. This new feature allows teams to move beyond static reports and query their data in real-time, enabling them to quickly identify emerging patterns and trends. With AI Analytics, security teams can surface the underlying causes of alert spikes and understand where to focus their efforts.

ZeroFox AI Analytics replaces the traditional workflow of manual exports and scheduled summaries with an interactive analytics experience. The platform includes features such as the Scout AI Assistant, which allows teams to ask plain-language questions and receive instant charts and insights. Additionally, AI Analytics connects the dots across alert data to surface underlying trends and patterns, enabling smarter decision-making. The platform also includes automated executive reporting, which allows teams to schedule recurring deliveries of personalized reports to leadership inboxes.

The ZeroFox AI Analytics platform includes interactive dashboards, data explorer workbooks, and enterprise sharing tools, providing a single source of truth for all users. The platform also offers custom branding capabilities, allowing teams to format dashboards and reports to corporate standards. With AI Analytics, security teams can independently validate the impact of their programs and take action on their findings. The feature is available now, providing security teams with a powerful tool to enhance their threat detection and response capabilities.
