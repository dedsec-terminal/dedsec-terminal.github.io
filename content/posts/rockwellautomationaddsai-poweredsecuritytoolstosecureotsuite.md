Rockwell Automation has enhanced its SecureOT solution suite with three new offerings: OT Cybersecurity Assessment Suite, SecureOT Platform Managed Services, and Managed Secure Remote Access (MSRA). These additions aim to help industrial enterprises strengthen their cybersecurity without compromising production. The SecureOT suite leverages artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning models to identify vulnerabilities, automate efficiency, and provide advanced intelligence to support analysis and risk understanding.

The OT Cybersecurity Assessment Suite features a modular approach tailored to industrial environments, combining data collection with business-ready insights to deliver a clear path to building resilience. The assessment offerings utilize proprietary AI and machine learning models to streamline analysis and standardize evaluation. Meanwhile, the SecureOT Platform Managed Services provide continuous, professionally managed risk prioritization, including managed platform updates, asset discovery, and technical account management.

The Managed Secure Remote Access (MSRA) service connects OT assets, reducing risks and improving efficiency in a vendor-neutral environment. The service enables faster troubleshooting and safer collaboration with vendors, with turnkey deployment, identity security, and reduced workload for internal teams. Additionally, Rockwell has introduced a refreshed OT Cybersecurity Policy & Procedures offering, providing a comprehensive set of documents to guide and strengthen security programs across the OT environment, aligned with international standards and best practices.
