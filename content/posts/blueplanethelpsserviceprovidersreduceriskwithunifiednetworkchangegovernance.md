Blue Planet has introduced a new solution, Blue Planet Configuration and Change Management (CCM), to help service providers reduce risk in their network operations. CCM is designed to unify device configuration, change, and lifecycle management across multi-vendor networks, replacing fragmented tools and manual processes with AI-driven workflows. This solution aims to reduce the risk of outages and strengthen the foundation for autonomous networking.

The need for CCM arises from the growing complexity of networks, where configuration errors and unmanaged changes are a leading cause of outages. Service providers must manage simultaneous manual and AI-driven automated changes across multi-vendor environments, often with limited visibility and fragmented control. CCM provides a real-time view of network state and activity, enabling service providers to safely scale automation and AI-driven operations. It also closes the automation loop by validating and tracking every change, reducing operational risk and strengthening network reliability.

The key capabilities of CCM include centralized configuration and change visibility, automated configuration drift detection, software image and device lifecycle management, and AI-enabled risk assessment. By embedding governance directly into network operations, CCM helps service providers realize the operational benefits of AI-driven automation with trust and confidence. According to industry experts, autonomy in network operations depends on building trust in the agents and systems empowered to change the network, and CCM provides a unified governance layer for network changes to achieve this goal.
