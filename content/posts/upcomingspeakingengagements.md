The author has a series of upcoming speaking engagements scheduled for June and July 2026, with events taking place in Europe and North America. The next day, they will speak at the Digital Humanism Conference and give a fireside chat for Epicenter Works, both in Vienna. They will also participate in a virtual panel discussion at Quantum.Tech World in Boston, Massachusetts, via Zoom, on June 26, 2026.

The author's speaking engagements will continue in the following weeks, with talks scheduled at Czech Technical University in Prague, Czechia, on June 29, 2026, and the Nuremberg Digital Festival in Nuremburg, Germany, on July 1, 2026. A final engagement is scheduled for September 30-October 1, 2026, at CanSecWest 2026 in Vancouver, Canada, although the exact time of the talk has not been determined. The list of speaking engagements is subject to update and can be found on the author's webpage.
