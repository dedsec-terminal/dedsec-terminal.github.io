Infosecurity Europe 2026 was a recent cybersecurity event held in London from June 2 to 4. The conference brought together various industry professionals and vendors to showcase the latest advancements in cybersecurity.

Help Net Security attended the event, providing a firsthand look at the conference. The event featured a range of vendors, including well-known companies such as Microsoft and Qualys, as well as other notable cybersecurity firms. These vendors showcased their products and services, highlighting the latest innovations in the field.

Some of the other vendors that participated in the event included JupiterOne, Menlo Security, Cato Networks, and Netskope, among others. The conference provided a platform for these companies to demonstrate their solutions and connect with industry professionals, promoting collaboration and knowledge-sharing in the cybersecurity community.
