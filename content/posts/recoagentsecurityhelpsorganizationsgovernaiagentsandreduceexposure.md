Reco has introduced Reco Agent Security, a solution that helps organizations govern AI agents and reduce exposure to security risks. AI agents operate across connected applications and workflows, accessing sensitive data and taking autonomous actions, which can lead to unintended use, data exposure, and process disruption. Reco Agent Security provides security teams with visibility and control across the environments where AI agents operate, enabling them to identify and mitigate potential risks.

The solution offers advanced capabilities such as agent discovery and inventory, identity and ownership mapping, permissions and scope analysis, activity and behavioral monitoring, and connectivity and blast-radius mapping. These features help security teams understand what agents can do, who owns them, and what they can reach, allowing them to prioritize risk and take precise remediation steps. Reco Agent Security also provides risk prioritization based on operating context, scoring and prioritizing agent risk to help security teams focus on the most critical exposures.

Reco Agent Security is built on the Reco Graph, which maps and monitors enterprise interactions, and is used by Fortune 500 organizations. The solution provides operational control over agents, enabling security teams to eliminate orphaned, unmanaged, and unowned agents, reduce excessive access, and remediate exposure without disrupting business processes. By providing context, coverage, and control, Reco Agent Security helps organizations protect their production environments from agent risk without slowing down AI adoption, according to Ofer Klein, CEO of Reco.
