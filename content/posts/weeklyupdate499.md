The author is developing a fondness for Bruce, an AI assistant, and has had an epiphany about its capabilities. They realize that Bruce is not just limited to auto-responding to tickets on its own, but can also be effective with a little human assistance.

The author and Charlotte experimented with this approach, responding to specific tickets that required additional information. By providing Bruce with a small amount of extra context, such as the number of domains being monitored, they were able to construct a suitable reply. This allowed Bruce to "own" the ticket and respond effectively.

The author believes that the ideal approach may be to use Bruce for auto-replies on straightforward issues, while leveraging human input for more complex tickets. This hybrid approach could be the "sweet spot" for maximizing the effectiveness of the AI assistant, allowing it to handle a wide range of tasks with minimal human intervention.
