Socure has introduced Remote Verifier, a new identity verification tool within its RiskOS platform. This tool is designed to help organizations verify identities that require additional review, reducing manual effort and minimizing friction for applicants. With an accuracy rate of over 99%, Socure's AI-powered document verification solution can instantly verify most identities, leaving fewer than 1% of individuals requiring additional scrutiny.

The Remote Verifier is equipped with features such as uploaded identity documents, real-time fraud intelligence, and risk indicators, enabling live agents to make informed decisions. This solution is particularly useful for public sector organizations, where fraud can put taxpayer dollars at risk and undermine government program integrity. By providing live agents with the necessary data and insights, Socure's Remote Verifier enables faster, more accurate, and higher-confidence identity decisions.

The Remote Verifier offers a single platform for scheduling, managing appointments, and overseeing the identity verification process, reducing the need for in-person and call-center verification. This solution has been praised by partners, such as Allocore, which has integrated Remote Verifier into its platform to support modernized loan and grant programs across the federal government. By blocking hundreds of millions of dollars in fraudulent activity each month, Socure's Remote Verifier is helping to transform critical federal systems while improving access for legitimate applicants.
