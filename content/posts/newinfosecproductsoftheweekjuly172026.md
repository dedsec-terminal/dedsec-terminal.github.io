Here are 3 concise paragraphs summarizing the new infosec products of the week:

This week saw the release of several new infosec products from prominent companies. Polygraf AI introduced Meeting Guard, a real-time AI fraud detection solution for enterprise meetings. This tool joins virtual meetings as a participant and provides near-real-time security analysis to attendees, detecting deepfakes, verifying voices, and flagging potential PII leaks. It also generates secure meeting notes and summaries without transmitting external data.

Nudge Security and Lineation.ai also announced new products. Nudge Security's new agentic capabilities help security teams detect and remediate malicious OAuth grants and browser extensions, which are growing attack surfaces in the enterprise. Lineation.ai, on the other hand, launched a comprehensive agentic security platform that focuses on runtime security for autonomous AI agents. The platform delivers a Zero Trust unified control plane and a lightweight endpoint daemon to secure AI agents directly at execution.

Cloudflare also released a new product, Precursor, a next-generation behavioral validation engine for bot management. Precursor uses continuous behavioral analysis to detect advanced bots, monitoring entire user sessions in real-time to catch automation. Unlike traditional CAPTCHAs, Precursor analyzes ongoing interactions to improve detection precision without interrupting legitimate users. These new products demonstrate the ongoing innovation in the infosec industry, addressing emerging threats and providing organizations with new tools to protect themselves.
