ValorC3 Data Centers has announced the general availability of its Backup as a Service (BaaS) offering, a fully managed solution that protects SaaS data from deletion, corruption, or ransomware attacks. The service provides immutable cloud backups for critical applications such as Microsoft 365, Entra ID, and Salesforce. This move addresses a common misconception that SaaS vendors provide backup services, when in reality, they prioritize service uptime over data recovery.

Many companies have experienced cloud security issues, with 80% of organizations facing at least one incident and 45% of data breaches originating from cloud environments. ValorC3's BaaS solution fills the gap in data protection, acknowledging that customers are responsible for safeguarding their own data. The company manages customers' data end-to-end, configuring and monitoring backup jobs, testing recovery, and building a protection strategy. By leveraging its partnership with Veeam, ValorC3 provides a reliable and secure backup solution.

ValorC3's BaaS solution ensures that backups are written once and cannot be altered or deleted, providing a clean recovery point even in the event of a ransomware attack. This allows customers to quickly revert to a clean copy of their data, minimizing downtime and potential losses. By outsourcing data protection to ValorC3, customers can focus on their core business, knowing that their critical data is safe and recoverable. The company's managed approach and immutable backups provide a robust defense against data loss, turning potential outages into non-events and saving customers from significant financial and reputational damage.
