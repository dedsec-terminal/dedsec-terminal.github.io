The Bangladesh government has joined the list of governments using Have I Been Pwned's (HIBP) free service. This marks the 43rd government to onboard with HIBP. The BGD e-GOV CIRT department will now have access to the service.

Through HIBP, the Bangladesh government will be able to query all their government domains via API and monitor them for potential breaches. This will enable them to identify and respond quickly to any incidents that may compromise their digital assets. The service will help protect government email addresses from exposure in data breaches.

By joining HIBP, Bangladesh is taking a proactive step to safeguard its public sector digital assets. The country is now part of a growing list of national governments using HIBP to enhance their cybersecurity measures. HIBP looks forward to supporting Bangladesh's efforts to identify and respond to potential breaches, ultimately strengthening their digital security.
