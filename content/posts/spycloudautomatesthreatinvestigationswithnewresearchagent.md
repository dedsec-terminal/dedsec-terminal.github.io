**Introduction to SpyCloud Research Agent**
SpyCloud has launched a new conversational AI investigation agent, SpyCloud Research Agent, which automates threat investigations in its Cybercrime Investigations console. This agent eliminates the need for manual pivot work, allowing security practitioners to focus on higher-level tasks. By providing a subject, hypothesis, or batch of assets, the Research Agent plans the investigation, sequences pivots, and returns finished intelligence, streamlining the investigation process.

**How SpyCloud Research Agent Works**
The Research Agent operates across three layers: investigative context, expert-level reasoning, and analyst-ready outputs. It starts by connecting related identity artifacts, then plans the investigation by reasoning about the user's goals and deciding which pivots to run. The agent returns findings in a format suitable for the investigation, such as a narrative summary or prioritized escalation recommendations. Unlike other tools, SpyCloud Research Agent thinks through the investigation before responding, accepting natural-language prompts and correlating across multiple inputs simultaneously.

**Benefits and Deliverables of SpyCloud Research Agent**
The Research Agent delivers several benefits, including the ability to skip query syntax and describe investigations in plain language, empowering every analyst to operate at a senior level, and providing a full identity picture. It also triggers holistic identity matching, surfaces personal accounts and device records, and provides evidence behind every finding. By automating threat investigations, SpyCloud Research Agent helps close the gap in cybercrime investigations, allowing analysts to refine questions, pursue new leads, and build on prior findings without starting over. This latest enhancement completes the trilogy of SpyCloud's Cybercrime Investigations, following the introduction of IDLink and AI Insights.
