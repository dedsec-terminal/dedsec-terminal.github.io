A recent cybersecurity incident involved a group of amateur sleuths on Discord gaining unauthorized access to Anthropic's Mythos Preview AI model. Despite Anthropic's efforts to restrict access, the group used straightforward detective work to gain access to the tool, including examining data from a recent breach of an AI training startup and utilizing existing permissions to access other Anthropic models. Fortunately, the group has only used the access to build simple websites, rather than exploiting the model's capabilities for malicious purposes.

In other cybersecurity news, researchers have warned about the vulnerability of telecom protocols, including Signaling System 7 (SS7), which can be exploited for surreptitious surveillance. Citizen Lab revealed that at least two surveillance vendors have used these vulnerabilities to spy on targets, tracking the location of high-profile individuals' phones. Additionally, the US Department of Justice has charged two Chinese men with allegedly managing a scam compound in Myanmar and seeking to open another in Cambodia, highlighting the growing crackdown on human-trafficking-fueled scam compounds in Southeast Asia.

The sale of sensitive health information has also been a concern, with over 500,000 UK health records found to be listed for sale on Alibaba. The data leak involved a breach of contract by three scientific research institutions, which had shared the health data with UK Biobank for medical research purposes. Meanwhile, Apple has released a security update to fix a bug that allowed the FBI to access push notifications from Signal, including encrypted messages. The update addresses a logging issue and improves data redaction, but users are still advised to change their notification settings to minimize potential exposure.

The incidents highlight the ongoing challenges in cybersecurity, from the exploitation of vulnerabilities in AI models and telecom protocols to the sale of sensitive health information and the potential for law enforcement to access encrypted messages. As cybersecurity threats continue to evolve, it is essential for individuals and organizations to remain vigilant and take proactive measures to protect their sensitive information. By staying informed about the latest cybersecurity news and updates, individuals can take steps to minimize their risk of exposure and stay safe online.
