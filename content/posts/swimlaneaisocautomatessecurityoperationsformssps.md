Swimlane has launched Swimlane AI SOC for MSSPs, a platform designed to automate security operations for managed security service providers (MSSPs). Unlike other AI SOC providers that are moving into managed services and competing with MSSPs, Swimlane is taking a collaborative approach. The platform empowers MSSPs to keep their customer relationships, data, and ownership of the services they deliver, while Swimlane focuses on providing agentic AI automation to support them.

The Swimlane AI SOC for MSSPs aims to improve the key metrics that MSSP leaders track, including margin and analyst capacity. The platform automates alert handling, allowing analysts to support more clients without adding headcount, and drives down the price per case while improving customer outcomes. It also replaces costly, one-off professional services builds with a standardized operating model that accelerates onboarding, improves analyst efficiency, and scales managed security services without scaling headcount.

The platform offers several new capabilities, including cross-tenant threat intelligence, agentic AI triage and investigation, and a central command center that aggregates prioritized cases from every connected client tenant in real-time. Additionally, it provides MSSP portfolio analytics and integrates with existing ITSM and CRM tools, such as ServiceNow and Jira. With Swimlane AI SOC, MSSPs can scale their operations with greater precision and efficiency, while maintaining control over their customer relationships and data. According to Swimlane's CEO, Cody Cornell, the platform is designed to help MSSPs cost-effectively adopt AI and automation to provide best-in-class outcomes for their customers.
