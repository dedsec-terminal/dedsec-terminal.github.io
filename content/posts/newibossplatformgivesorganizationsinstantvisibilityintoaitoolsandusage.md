iboss has launched the AI Security Platform, a new service that provides organizations with instant visibility into the AI tools being used by their employees. The platform is free to sign up and deploy, and it can provide a complete AI footprint within hours. This is particularly important as AI adoption accelerates, and security teams struggle to keep pace with the use of unauthorized AI tools, which can lead to data leaks and security breaches.

The AI Security Platform tracks AI tool usage in real-time, covering both browser-based tools and desktop applications, and classifies each tool by risk. It also provides a searchable history of prompts and responses, attributed to individual users. For organizations that want to go beyond visibility, paid tiers of the platform offer additional features, such as the ability to enforce AI policy, prevent data leaks, and govern AI agents. These features include default Allow, Block, or Redirect policies, in-the-moment coaching messages, and copy, paste, download, and upload controls.

The AI Security Platform is designed to be scalable and easy to use, with a simple model that allows organizations to start for free and then apply controls at their own pace. It can be used by individual users or global enterprises, and it meets regulated-industry standards, including HIPAA, PCI, and FedRAMP. The platform's goal is to provide organizations with a simple and effective way to see and control AI usage, without having to choose between ease of adoption and audit readiness. With the AI Security Platform, organizations can discover shadow AI, control AI costs, prevent data leaks, secure AI agents, and stay compliant with regulatory requirements.
