Veritone has introduced Veritone Assess, an AI-powered data analysis solution designed to help public sector agencies streamline evidence analysis and compliance reviews. This solution automatically evaluates reports, witness statements, and other evidentiary materials against policies, regulations, and investigative criteria, reducing the time required for manual reviews and improving decision-making speed and accuracy. By transforming unstructured information into actionable intelligence, Assess helps law enforcement, military, and compliance professionals accelerate investigations, strengthen oversight, and improve case outcomes.

Veritone Assess is available as a standalone application and is planned for future integration with Veritone Investigate, expanding Veritone's AI-powered ecosystem for investigative and compliance workflows. The solution features automated deviation analysis, event timeline generation, flexible rule sets, intelligence summary, and a custom chatbot, all within a secure and controlled AI environment. This enables public sector agencies to identify inconsistencies, missing information, and critical intelligence gaps, and to generate actionable leads and advance cases. The Cold Case Foundation is already leveraging Veritone Assess to process decades of unstructured case files and disparate evidence in a matter of minutes.

The key benefits of Veritone Assess include accelerated analysis and comparison of data sets, mitigation of operational and legal risks, and significant speedup of workflow, allowing investigators to focus on public safety. By automating the most time-consuming aspects of evidence review and compliance checking, Veritone is enabling operational efficiency, transparency, and accountability in government investigations and audits. With Veritone Assess, public sector agencies can transform vast amounts of complex data into a strategic advantage, helping them to do more with less and improve case outcomes. The solution is designed to support data security, privacy, and compliance for sensitive public sector investigations and evidence management.
