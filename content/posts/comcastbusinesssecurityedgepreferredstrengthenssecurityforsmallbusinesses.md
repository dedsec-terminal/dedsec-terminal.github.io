Comcast Business has introduced SecurityEdge Preferred, a network-native cybersecurity solution designed to strengthen security for small businesses. This advanced solution is built directly into the Comcast Business network, allowing for activation in minutes without the need for additional hardware or complex security tools. By intercepting threats at the network edge, SecurityEdge Preferred provides an architectural advantage that other products cannot match.

The need for robust cybersecurity is evident, with Comcast Business's 2025 Cybersecurity Threat Report revealing a record-high threat environment for small businesses. Outside research also confirms that small businesses are increasingly targeted by cyberattacks, with 43% of all attacks now targeting these organizations. Furthermore, financial losses from breaches have doubled in two years, with damages often exceeding $500,000. SecurityEdge Preferred aims to address this issue by providing enterprise-grade security that is simple to activate and manage, without requiring a dedicated security staff or complex deployments.

SecurityEdge Preferred offers a range of features, including real-time monitoring of incoming and outgoing traffic, AI-powered threat intelligence, and advanced filtering to block malicious IP addresses and restrict traffic from high-risk regions. The solution is available for $40 per month for Comcast Business Internet customers with speeds below 1 Gbps and $60 per month for Gig+ customers, with no per-seat licenses or annual subscription fees required. With its integrated approach to connectivity and cybersecurity, SecurityEdge Preferred provides small businesses with a powerful and dependable solution to protect against cyber threats.
