Intezer has introduced Custom Agents, a new feature that enables security teams to build and automate custom security tasks within the Intezer platform. This capability allows teams to create AI agents that can perform unique and repetitive tasks, such as generating custom incident reports, tuning detection rules, and documenting investigation outcomes. By automating these tasks, security teams can free up time and resources to focus on more complex and high-priority threats.

The launch of Custom Agents builds on Intezer's core approach of using autonomous agents to triage, investigate, and respond to alerts around the clock. With Custom Agents, customers can now automate additional investigation work, report generation, and other recurring SOC routines that are specific to their environment. This feature has been developed in response to the growing volume and complexity of modern threats, which can no longer be managed through manual alert handling or one-off automation.

Custom Agents are easy to create using natural language, and can be integrated with a range of tools, including SIEM, EDR, and identity management systems. They can be scheduled to run at specific times or in response to certain events, and can take action by updating cases, commenting on reports, and emailing finished reports. According to Itai Tevet, CEO of Intezer, Custom Agents give customers the power to automate their unique workflows, running them precisely how they choose, and ensuring seamless integration and performance with their existing SOC operations.
