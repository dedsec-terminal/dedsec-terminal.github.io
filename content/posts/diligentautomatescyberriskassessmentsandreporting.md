Diligent has introduced Diligent Cyber Risk Management, a platform designed to help organizations manage cybersecurity risk in a business context. This solution aims to automate cyber risk assessments and reporting, reducing the time required from weeks to hours. It links cyber threats to strategic objectives, critical business processes, and board-level oversight, enabling organizations to prioritize security investments based on business impact.

The platform brings together technical security data and business context in a single platform, supporting end-to-end cyber risk workflows. Key features include AI-powered cyber risk assessments, risk scoring tied to critical assets and processes, centralized risk remediation and IT compliance, and board-ready cyber risk reporting. These features provide organizations with a clear picture of their cyber risk posture, allowing them to make informed decisions and prioritize security investments effectively.

Diligent Cyber Risk Management is set to be available in summer 2026 and is expected to be transformative for organizations. It will provide a unified view of governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) across enterprise risk management, audit, and cyber risk. The platform will help organizations avoid conflicting risk narratives and provide a single source of truth for the C-suite and the board. By automating cyber risk assessments and reporting, Diligent Cyber Risk Management will enable organizations to prioritize security decisions that matter and clarify the impact of cyber risk to management and the board.
