Red Sift has partnered with GMO GlobalSign to simplify email authentication and Brand Indicators for Message Identification (BIMI) adoption. This partnership provides organizations with a direct path from email authentication to verified brand visibility in the inbox, eliminating the need to source DMARC tooling separately. Red Sift's OnDMARC is now available through GMO GlobalSign, enabling secure outbound email protection and BIMI activation through a single trusted provider.

The partnership removes administrative overhead and coordination headaches, allowing customers to achieve DMARC enforcement, VMC setup, and BIMI activation through a single path. With OnDMARC, customers can reach DMARC enforcement in as little as 6-8 weeks, and then obtain a Verified Mark Certificate (VMC) or Common Mark Certificate (CMC) from GMO GlobalSign. This enables organizations to display a verified brand logo next to messages in recipients' inboxes, increasing email open rates by 39% and adding a blue verification tick to confirm domain authentication.

The partnership is available to customers outside of Japan, primarily in Europe and the Americas. To check readiness for BIMI, GMO GlobalSign customers can use Red Sift's free BIMI checker, which provides a breakdown of email authentication setup, DMARC policy level, and BIMI status in seconds. By simplifying the process, Red Sift and GMO GlobalSign aim to help organizations achieve secure outbound email protection and verified brand visibility, aligning security and marketing goals.
