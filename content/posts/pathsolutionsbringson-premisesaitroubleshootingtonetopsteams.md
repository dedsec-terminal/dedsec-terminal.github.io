PathSolutions has introduced TotalView AI, a new capability within its TotalView platform that provides AI-driven troubleshooting for NetOps teams. This on-premises solution analyzes network data locally, eliminating the need to send limited datasets to the cloud, which can introduce latency, increase costs, and restrict visibility. By operating entirely on-premises, TotalView AI can analyze a broader and richer set of network data, enabling faster and more accurate insights.

The on-premises approach of TotalView AI offers several advantages, including complete data visibility, real-time analysis with low latency, and no data movement constraints. This allows organizations to keep sensitive network and operational data within their boundaries, ensuring data sovereignty and control. TotalView AI also supports air-gapped deployment, making it suitable for high-security and regulated environments. With its ability to analyze comprehensive telemetry, including SNMP, flow data, and device-level metrics, TotalView AI provides deeper insights and more accurate conclusions.

TotalView AI's key capabilities include AI-powered root-cause analysis, correlation across complete datasets, and faster mean time to resolution (MTTR). It enables less-experienced engineers to diagnose complex issues with confidence, reducing troubleshooting time from hours to minutes. By working with a complete, high-fidelity view of the network, TotalView AI ensures more accurate conclusions and fewer false positives. According to Tim Titus, CTO at PathSolutions, "AI is only as effective as the data behind it," and TotalView AI's on-premises approach addresses the challenges of incomplete data and lack of correlation, making it a powerful tool for NetOps teams.
