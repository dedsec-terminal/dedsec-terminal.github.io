The CIS SecureSuite Platform is a new tool designed to simplify security management for organizations. It provides a centralized interface for managing security posture, assessing risks, and implementing secure configurations. The platform is built exclusively for CIS SecureSuite Members and consolidates tools such as CIS CSAT Pro and CIS-CAT Pro Dashboard into a single interface. This allows members to assess, align, and act on their security posture with integrated workflows that simplify compliance and strengthen decision-making.

The CIS SecureSuite Platform offers several key benefits, including centralized visibility and management, and accelerated compliance and reporting. Members can use the platform to identify gaps in their security posture, track progress, and prioritize actions based on risk. The platform also provides built-in tools and automated assessments to demonstrate alignment with industry-recognized standards and regulatory requirements, such as the CIS Critical Security Controls and ISO/IEC 27001:2022. Additionally, the platform includes features such as CIS-CAT Pro Assessor for audit reporting and GPO-to-Intune mappings to simplify deployment and policy management during cloud transitions.

CIS SecureSuite Membership provides practical applications and use cases for various industries, including regional banks, credit unions, healthcare, investment firms, insurance providers, state government IT, K-12 school districts, and local municipalities. Members can use the platform to secure remote workstations, meet compliance requirements, and generate audit-ready reports. For a limited time, CIS SecureSuite is offering exclusive multi-year membership discounts, allowing organizations to simplify security management and compliance while saving on costs. By becoming a CIS SecureSuite Member, organizations can harden their systems faster, simplify compliance, and improve their overall security posture.
