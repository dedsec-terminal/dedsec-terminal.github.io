Here are three concise paragraphs summarizing the article:

Most organizations have come to realize that endpoint protection alone is no longer sufficient, leading to a rapid adoption of endpoint detection and response (EDR) in recent years. However, simply owning EDR capabilities does not automatically create operational cyber resilience. Many organizations struggle to fully operationalize these capabilities due to overwhelmed security teams, lengthy investigations, and stretched response capacity. As threats become faster and more sophisticated, organizations are recognizing that visibility alone is no longer enough.

The main barriers to fully leveraging EDR include too many alerts, insufficient investigation capacity, limited time for continuous monitoring, skills shortages, and operational fatigue. As a result, organizations often have strong visibility but inconsistent response maturity, creating a gap between security capability and security outcomes. The rise of AI-enabled attacks has further increased operational pressure on already overstretched teams, with 67% of organizations reporting an increase in such attacks. Attackers are now using legitimate administrative tools and trusted processes to evade detection, making purely reactive security postures inadequate.

To address these challenges, organizations can consider dynamic hardening and managed detection and response (MDR) to elevate their security without adding complexity. Bitdefender's GravityZone PHASR and MDR capabilities can help reduce exploitable conditions, provide continuous operational capacity, and create a layered operational model on top of EDR. By operationalizing their existing EDR investment with proactive hardening and MDR, organizations can achieve measurable security and business outcomes, including reduced risk, faster detection and containment, and improved cyber resilience. The future of cyber resilience lies in operationalized security, which requires proactive reduction of exploitable conditions, continuous operational response capability, and sustainable workflows for lean teams.
