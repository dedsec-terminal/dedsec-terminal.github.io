SuperOps and Guardz have announced a strategic partnership to offer a bundled product for managed service providers (MSPs). The package combines professional services automation (PSA), remote monitoring and management (RMM), mobile device management (MDM), and security operations into one purchase. This integration aims to simplify the workflow for MSPs, who often have to navigate multiple disconnected tools and workflows, resulting in significant operational costs.

The bundled product brings together SuperOps' IT operations and endpoint management capabilities with Guardz' security operations, providing MSPs with a single system for service delivery and endpoint management. The combined product offers context, visibility, and control to manage and protect customers from one connected system. This partnership is driven by market pressures, including the need for MSPs to manage risk and technology, as well as the increasing importance of AI readiness and security readiness.

The bundle is available immediately to MSPs globally and is priced lower than traditional multi-vendor stacks. According to the companies, this partnership will help MSPs replace complexity with clarity and build a stronger foundation for the AI era. The CEOs of SuperOps and Guardz believe that running operations and security on one foundation gives MSPs the visibility to protect every client and the credibility to guide them into AI. An MSP founder also noted that a simpler stack makes a business easier to scale, as complexity scales faster than revenue.
