Intruder has launched AI-powered, on-demand penetration testing for web applications, providing organizations with a secure and efficient way to identify security vulnerabilities. The platform allows companies to connect their codebases via GitHub or GitLab and automatically scope and launch penetration tests in minutes, with results and audit-ready reporting available in hours. This innovation addresses the need for more frequent pentesting, as annual tests are no longer sufficient to keep up with the rapid pace of code deployments and the increasing use of AI-assisted coding.

The need for continuous app testing has become more pressing, with 49% of security leaders citing AI and automation as their top investment priority for 2026. The use of AI-assisted coding assistants has accelerated the introduction of new application vulnerabilities, creating a growing need for autonomous security solutions. Intruder's AI Pentesting is designed to deliver the depth of a manual, human-led engagement in an automated framework, providing critical capabilities such as white-box pentests, built by CREST-certified pentesters, and audit-level reporting.

Intruder's AI Pentesting is a game-changer for security teams, providing on-demand penetration testing at a fraction of the cost of traditional manual engagements. The platform delivers results in hours, rather than weeks or months, and is affordable for small and medium-sized businesses. According to Chris Wallis, CEO at Intruder, the cost and timeline of traditional pentesting are no longer viable in today's accelerated threat environment. With Intruder's AI Pentesting, organizations can ensure continuous defense and keep their platforms permanently hardened, as demonstrated by Yembo, a global AI platform that uses Intruder's AI pentesting to bridge the gap between annual human-led assessments.
