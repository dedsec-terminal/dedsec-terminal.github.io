The author of Weekly Update 498 spent a significant amount of time discussing the challenges of chasing overdue invoices. A customer, who remains unnamed, had accumulated invoices over 6 months past due, despite having a 30-day payment term. On average, this customer paid their invoices 80 days late.

The author was frustrated not only with the late payments but also with the CEO's response to their concerns. Instead of apologizing for the delay or making amends, the CEO took issue with the author's tone. The author found this reaction to be dismissive of basic business etiquette and the importance of paying suppliers on time.

In response to this experience, the author and Copilot developed a Xero integration over the weekend to prevent similar situations in the future. This new system will ensure that unpaid invoices are addressed promptly, and services may be suspended if payments are not made on time. The author warns that if the HIBP enterprise service is no longer functioning, it may be due to unpaid invoices, and encourages customers to pay their bills in a timely manner.
