AppOmni has launched Marlin AI, a revolutionary solution that automates SaaS threat analysis, triage, and remediation at scale. Marlin AI leverages AppOmni's deep SaaS application observability to deliver autonomous AI-powered SaaS security, enabling security teams to move beyond manual event and indicator correlation to autonomous triaging and guided remediation. By reducing the time spent on investigating threats, alerts, and warnings, security teams can now focus on more critical tasks.

Marlin AI is designed to address the complexities of SaaS environments, which general AI tools often struggle to navigate. With its holistic approach, Marlin AI looks deeper into SaaS environments than any other solution on the market, providing insights and guidance to help security teams understand what matters most and take action faster. Autonomous AI-powered SaaS security is a model where AI systems actively perform security tasks instead of waiting for human input, making it the only scalable way to keep pace with the overwhelming volume of SaaS and security data generated across complex environments.

Marlin AI improves security team productivity by reducing the mean time to investigate (MTTI) and mean time to remediate (MTTR) for security teams, removing repetitive SaaS investigation tasks and the dependency on manual workflows. Key capabilities of Marlin AI include AI-driven alert correlation, automated security investigations, step-by-step remediation guidance, pre-built playbooks, and deep SaaS analysis. With Marlin AI, AppOmni is the only enterprise-grade SaaS security solution with autonomous AI security functionality on the market today, providing organizations with a superior path to operational maturity and a powerful example of how purpose-built AI can deliver meaningful security outcomes at scale.
