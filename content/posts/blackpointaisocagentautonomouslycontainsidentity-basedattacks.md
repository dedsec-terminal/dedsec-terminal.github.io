Blackpoint Cyber has introduced the Blackpoint AI SOC Agent, an autonomous response capability for identity threat detection and response (ITDR). This AI-powered agent can contain credential-based attacks in under two minutes on average, and as quickly as 21 seconds. The agent uses a hybrid model that combines AI with human expertise to target Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace accounts.

The need for such a capability is growing, with identity-based attacks increasing by 32% in the first half of 2025, according to Microsoft. Attackers are using large language models to target identities at an unprecedented pace. Blackpoint's AI SOC Agent was trained on years of SOC analyst decisions, forensic evidence, and telemetry from nearly a million accounts, and was rigorously validated to ensure high-confidence threat containment.

The Blackpoint AI SOC Agent brings the company's frontline security expertise to customers, delivering machine-speed threat containment. According to Gagan Singh, CEO of Blackpoint Cyber, the company's SOC is a key reason why AI works in cybersecurity, and human judgment will always be necessary to secure outcomes. The agent has received positive feedback from customers, such as STF Consulting, who appreciate the confidence that comes with having a trusted extension of their security team.
