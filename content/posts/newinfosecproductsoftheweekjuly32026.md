Here are 3 concise paragraphs summarizing the new infosec products of the week:

Digi International has launched DANI, a purpose-built AI network operations agent that automates network diagnostics and device management. Embedded in Digi Remote Manager, DANI enables network operators to monitor and diagnose network issues, identify root causes, and receive recommended actions through a conversational interface. This new capability streamlines network management and improves overall efficiency.

Jamf and Netzilo have also introduced new products focused on AI governance and security. Jamf's AI Governance enables IT and security teams to discover and manage AI tools on Mac devices, enforcing policy controls and generating audit-ready reports. Netzilo's expanded AI agent governance capabilities provide runtime enforcement and threat detection across major platforms, including Amazon Bedrock AgentCore. These products help organizations detect and mitigate potential AI-related security risks.

iboss has launched a new AI Security Platform that provides organizations with instant visibility into AI tool usage, free of charge. The platform tracks prompts, sessions, users, and risk in real-time across various AI services, including ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, and desktop applications like Cursor. This platform helps organizations stay on top of AI tool usage and potential security risks, ensuring they can respond quickly to emerging threats. These new products demonstrate the growing focus on AI-related security and governance in the infosec industry.
