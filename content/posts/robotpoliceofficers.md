The Sacramento County Sheriff's Office has successfully used a drone to disarm a suspect in a recent operation. On June 22, a video was posted on the office's Instagram page showing an officer operating a drone to retrieve a knife from an armed suspect hiding in a house. The drone was equipped with a high-powered magnet that was used to grab the knife out of the suspect's hand.

The operation was carried out after the suspect failed to respond to negotiators, and the drone was deployed inside the residence to locate the suspect. The drone pilots were able to find the suspect hiding in a corner of the garage and used the magnet to disarm them. The video of the operation, set to the "Mission: Impossible" theme song, shows the intercepted knife spinning in the air as the drone carries it back to the deputies.

However, some have questioned the authenticity of the video, with one commenter suggesting that the suspect may have been unconscious or play-acting, as there was no visible sign of them tightening their grip on the knife. Despite this, the use of the drone in this operation marks a significant step towards the development of robot police officers, highlighting the potential for technology to be used in law enforcement to reduce risk and improve outcomes.
