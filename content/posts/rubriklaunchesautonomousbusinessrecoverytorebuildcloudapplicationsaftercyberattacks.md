Rubrik has launched Autonomous Business Recovery (ABR) for Cloud Applications, a cyber resilience solution that rebuilds cloud applications after cyberattacks. ABR, powered by Rubrik's Preemptive Recovery Engine, discovers applications and their dependencies, validates clean points, and pre-builds recovery plans during peacetime. This allows for rapid recovery when an attack occurs, restoring an organization's Minimum Viable Business (MVB) at machine speed.

The solution addresses the limitations of traditional backup tools, which only restore individual resources rather than the complex dependencies that make up a modern application. ABR for Cloud Applications provides a graphical dependency map, protects everything in one place, and orchestrates automatic recovery in the correct order. This ensures that an organization's MVB is completely restored during a cyber incident, including access to compute, data, networking, Identity and Access Management (IAM), and configurations.

According to Rubrik, 88% of leaders are concerned about meeting current recovery time objectives (RTOs) as agentic threats increase. ABR for Cloud Applications is a Cloud Application Infrastructure Recovery (CAIRS) solution that provides a comprehensive, preemptive approach to recovering the entire application environment. Gartner predicts that by 2030, 35% of organizations will utilize CAIRS solutions, up from less than 5% in 2026. With ABR, organizations can configure and validate full recovery during peacetime, allowing them to instantaneously kick off recovery when an attack happens, and minimizing the impact of cyber incidents.
