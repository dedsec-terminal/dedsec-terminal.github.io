Insight has introduced a managed security service called Insight Managed Exposure Defense, designed to help organizations identify and address vulnerabilities. This service aims to reduce exposure and implement protections without requiring lengthy procurement processes or multiple vendors. The need for such a service is driven by the increasing speed of AI-assisted exploit development, which has shortened the window for vulnerability exploitation from days to hours.

Insight Managed Exposure Defense is a bundled service that covers every stage of the AI-driven vulnerability response loop, including managed Continuous Threat Exposure Management, patch operations, software supply chain and open source software risk management, software developer outsourcing, and managed XDR (Extended Detection and Response). The service provides a real-time, risk-ranked exposure map, enterprise-scale patch operations, and 24x7x365 detection, triage, and response from a global Security Operations Center (SOC). This comprehensive approach enables organizations to respond quickly to emerging threats without being overwhelmed by the operational load.

The service is particularly suited for mid-market organizations that face the same threat landscape as larger enterprises but have more limited security budgets. Insight Managed Exposure Defense is aligned with various regulatory frameworks, including NIST CSF 2.0, NIST AI RMF, HIPAA, and SEC disclosure rules, ensuring that organizations can maintain compliance while protecting themselves against emerging threats. By outsourcing vulnerability management to Insight, organizations can focus on their core business while leaving the security operations to a trusted partner.
