eSentire has introduced new autonomous threat detection and response capabilities within its Atlas Platform, a unified AI platform that utilizes purpose-built AI Operatives. The Atlas Platform delivers adaptive AI infrastructure in a continuous security lifecycle, executing hundreds of thousands of autonomous investigations and responses across customer environments. This approach enables eSentire to operate as a human-on-the-loop, pairing agentic AI operatives with human-judgment controls to deliver expert-depth security outcomes at machine speed.

The Atlas Platform addresses the four primary gaps in modern enterprise security: attacker velocity, exploit acceleration, talent and tool gaps, and vendor lock-in. It seamlessly integrates into existing infrastructures without disrupting current technology investments, providing holistic protection across the entire digital estate. The platform's capabilities include continuous attacker view of exploitable exposure, offense-to-defense correlation, and evidence-based coverage gap recommendations. Additionally, eSentire offers customers rapid engagement times, autonomous signal triage, and swift signal to full threat context.

The enhanced Atlas Platform capabilities enable eSentire to provide controlled autonomy SecOps, expert-depth capability governed by engineered human controls. This approach allows defenders to keep pace with attackers, continuously and with judgment. With Atlas, eSentire's AI surfaces exposures that attackers would exploit, driving detection, response, and remediation in the customer's environment. The company's goal is to prevent business disruption, with customers measuring success by the number of attacks that never achieve disruption, rather than just alerts triaged or hours saved.
