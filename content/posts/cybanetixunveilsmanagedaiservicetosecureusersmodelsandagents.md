Cybanetix has launched its Managed AI Service, a comprehensive solution designed to secure users, models, and agents within an enterprise. The service combines technology from leading providers such as NOMA, SentinelOne, Microsoft, and Exabeam with Cybanetix's consultancy, managed services, and 24/7 Security Operations Centre (SOC) monitoring. This integrated approach provides a 360-degree view of the AI security ecosystem, enabling organizations to respond to alerts in under 15 minutes.

The Managed AI Service addresses the three key domains of AI risk: user behavior, AI governance, and embedded AI. It provides observability and exposure mapping, behavioral monitoring, runtime protection, and synthetic and adversarial testing of AI models. The service also includes AI risk assessment, technology deployment, AI posture management, managed detection and response, and continuous AI risk reporting. By leveraging the expertise of AI security specialists and the capabilities of the technology stack, the Managed AI Service helps organizations to discover and control AI tools, agents, and models, and to respond to AI-specific risks such as prompt abuse and model manipulation.

The Managed AI Service offers numerous benefits, including faster detection and response, integrated workflows and automation, and correlated threat intelligence. It also provides organizations with access to AI experts, eliminating the need to hire in-house specialists. According to Merlin Gillespie, CTO at Cybanetix, the Managed AI Service is a comprehensive solution that covers all aspects of AI security, providing a credible AI security program that meets the needs of CISOs and regulators. By addressing the growing risk of AI-related threats, the Managed AI Service helps organizations to protect their AI assets and maintain a secure AI posture.
