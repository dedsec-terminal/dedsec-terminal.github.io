Proton has launched Lumo 2.0, a significant upgrade to its zero-access encrypted AI assistant. This new version brings the assistant closer to frontier AI models with enhanced features such as new AI models, multimodal capabilities, memory, and improved web search. Lumo 2.0 is designed to provide users with a more advanced and secure AI experience without compromising on data privacy.

Lumo 2.0 offers various plans, including a free plan with core features, Lumo Plus with unlimited chats and advanced image generation, and Lumo Professional for organizations with secure collaboration features and team management. The assistant has two model types: Lumo 2.0 Lite for general questions and everyday tasks, and Lumo 2.0 Max for more complex and demanding tasks. Additionally, it features fast and thinking modes, allowing users to choose between rapid responses and more detailed answers.

The new version also includes features such as user-controlled memory, projects, and custom Lumos, which enable users to create tailored AI assistants for specific tasks. Multimodal capabilities allow the assistant to analyze and generate images, documents, and charts, while the upgraded web search provides live information and source citations. Proton's enterprise offering, Lumo for Business, provides centralized administration and privacy-focused safeguards for organizations, ensuring that conversations are protected with zero-access encryption and remain on the company's European infrastructure.
