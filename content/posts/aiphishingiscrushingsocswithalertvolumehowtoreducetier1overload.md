The rise of AI-powered phishing attacks has led to an overwhelming volume of alerts for Security Operations Centers (SOCs). Attackers can now create convincing emails, fake login pages, and tailored lures in minutes, making it difficult for Tier 1 teams to review and respond to each alert. As a result, critical threats can get buried in the queue, delaying response and increasing the risk of a costly incident. SOC leaders need to help their teams cut through the noise faster and catch the alerts that could turn into a serious incident.

The main challenges faced by Tier 1 teams include the increasing sophistication of phishing campaigns, which require more manual review and context checking. AI-driven changes, such as more lure variations, better impersonation, and personalized messages, make it harder for teams to rule out alerts quickly. Additionally, short-lived domains and uncertain cases lead to more time spent on each alert and more cases being pushed to Tier 2 for further review. To address this issue, Tier 1 teams need a way to investigate more alerts without spending extra time on repetitive steps or pushing every unclear case to senior teams.

To reduce Tier 1 overload, a faster workflow that combines automated checks, behavior-based visibility, and ready-made reports is necessary. Solutions like ANY.RUN's Interactive Sandbox provide Tier 1 teams with the evidence needed to reach a clear verdict sooner. This approach allows teams to open suspicious links in a real browser environment, interact with the page freely, and trace the full attack chain without putting company devices or infrastructure at risk. By providing full behavior visibility, automating repetitive investigation steps, and generating ready-made reports, Tier 1 teams can process more phishing alerts without adding more manual work, reducing the time real threats stay unresolved and making decisions based on evidence, not assumptions.
