PrivacyHawk has launched PrivacyHawk Enterprise, a solution designed to help organizations identify and eliminate shadow IT accounts, abandoned SaaS subscriptions, and forgotten third-party services that pose a cyber risk. These hidden accounts and services can quietly expose organizations to breach risk, creating an invisible attack surface that traditional security tools may not detect.

The PrivacyHawk Enterprise solution provides security teams with full visibility into their employees' external digital footprint, automating data deletion across thousands of third-party services. This reduces the exposure that existing tools may leave behind, minimizing the risk of sensitive company data being exposed in the event of a third-party breach. By eliminating unnecessary third-party accounts and services, organizations can lower their attack surface and reduce the risk of data exposure.

According to Aaron Mendes, CEO of PrivacyHawk, large organizations can have millions of unknown third-party shadow IT accounts, each posing a potential data exposure risk. PrivacyHawk Enterprise aims to address this issue by bringing automated data deletion capabilities to businesses, nonprofits, and government agencies. By reducing their digital footprint, organizations can better protect themselves against third-party breaches and minimize the consequences of a potential data exposure.
